The Moff Band

The smart wristband toy for kids (and adults with the imaginations of kids)

web design, front-end code, copywriting

Although the team behind the Moff band initially contacted me about writing copy for their new global website (after a tremendously successful Kickstarter in Japan), the project grew until I became not only the copywriter, but also the web designer and developer.

I was tasked with creating a rich, fun, responsive website that simply explained the toy concept and resonated with American audiences.
