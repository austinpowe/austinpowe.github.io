Surrounded by the Sound

A collaborative zine composed of 16 essays about the music that changed your life.

Because I was frustrated with the lack of venues for the kind of writing that I wanted to read, I created a zine—an anthology, of sorts—that put together the writing of 15 talented storytellers and music fans.

What resulted was a collection of essays focused on stories about bands that have shaped people's lives, the first moment someone found the album that became a piece of their childhood, and the connections we make in our brains between a single song and a time in our lives.

I put the concept together, put out a call for submissions, edited the anthology, designed the PDF, and hand-stitched a few copies so that each contributor could have his/her own physical copy. I'd love to make this an annual project, so look out for "Surrounded by the Sound 2" or "Surrounded by Another Sound" soon.
