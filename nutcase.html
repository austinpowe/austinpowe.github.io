Nutcase Helmets

I'm a nutcase.

Giving a group of amateur creatives the task of making helmets cool to 16-24 year olds sounds like a pretty sick joke. The punchline is that I spent a whole summer working on it and actually enjoyed it.

As part of the Portland Advertising Federation's 2013 COLABORATORY internship, I worked with a team called Bright Knight to get millennials to buy Nutcase helmets.

This is the end result: A complete media plan filled with research, insight, executions, and expected outcomes.
