Delta's Connecting Flights

Bringing long-separated siblings together by partnering with the Donor Sibling Registry.

art direction, illustration, motion design, video production

You know how it feels when you work with a group that gels so well that the room seems a little sunnier than normal? That's what working on this project with Madi McCallum and Ben Kraft was like.

A big-idea solution for a big brand and a big problem.


Although this was only a student idea, it became much more real after we released our video. Wendy Kramer, the founder of the Donor Sibling Registry, posted our video to Facebook and we received feedback from the very people who this plan would affect. The comments we heard were overwhelmingly positive, and our hunch was confirmed: the time is right for a brand to tackle this project.
